Before
Sharing was fragmented and frustrating.

Sharing presentations was fragmented and frustrating. Teams relied on manual file transfers, struggled with confusing permission settings, and constantly hit version conflicts — slowing down their workflow.

Process
Research, prototyping, and close collaboration with engineering.

I combined user interviews with quantitative data analysis to identify pain points, prototyped and tested solutions iteratively, and collaborated with engineering to bring the final design to life.

Experiments
A/B tests and usability sessions with real users.

I validated solutions through A/B tests and usability sessions with real users, testing new sharing workflows and permission controls to find what actually worked before committing to a final direction.

Final design
A clean, intuitive interface that simplifies sharing and collaboration.

The final design delivers a clean, intuitive interface that simplifies presentation sharing and collaboration. It balances powerful features with ease of use, ensuring teams can create, share, and work together seamlessly.

Wider impact
Insights from the redesign shaped Pitch's monetisation model.

The work went beyond UX. Insights from the redesign directly informed a full monetisation model revamp — reshaping how Pitch structured its free and paid tiers to drive team growth and upgrade intent.
